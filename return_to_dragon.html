Returning to the Dragon

You return to the dragon’s cave with a grim expression. It sleeps with its eyes closed, and using your sword, you cut its lips and attempt to retrieve the last drops of venom from its jaw.

It awakens suddenly, and recognizing you, starts roaring frighteningly. You attempt to dash out, but it spits out a green flame that strikes you on your back.

Weakened, you crawl out of the cave and roll into the river that runs nearby, panting. After a few moments, you open your eyes, looking up, and see the inside of the dragon’s mouth—until the blood from its lip blinds you.

BAD ENDING: YOU HAVE DIED!
